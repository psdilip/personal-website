Two days, over 25 miles walked, and most of downtown Atlanta covered on foot.

### Day 1

**Stats:** 15.5 miles walked

**Meals:** Waffle House for breakfast · Circle Poke for lunch · Bangkok Station for dinner · Airbnb overnight

**Stops:** The Georgia State Capitol, Memorial Drive Greenway, Columbia Tower, Grant Park, Krog Street Tunnel, the Martin Luther King Jr. birth home, the King Center, the MLK National Historical Park, the Mohandas Gandhi statue, the Jackson Street Bridge, the Olympic Rings on Capitol Ave, the Peachtree Fountains plaza, the Woodruff Park fountain, CNN Center, State Farm Arena, Georgia International Plaza, Mercedes-Benz Stadium, the Fountain of Rings, SkyView Atlanta, Centennial Olympic Park, the World of Coca-Cola, Georgia Tech, the Atlanta Humane Society, and the Millennium Gate Museum

### Day 2

**Stats:** 10.2 miles walked · overnight bus travel

**Meals:** Panera Bread for breakfast · Starbucks for dinner

**Stops:** The BeltLine Trail, the Atlanta Botanical Garden, Ponce City Market, The Oval, Piedmont Park, Historic Fourth Ward Park, Little Five Points, and Inman Park, with some of the city seen by train in between.
