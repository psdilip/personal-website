---

Nobody tells you that the hardest part of starting over isn't the learning. It's the identity. For a while you are visibly a beginner again, and after years of being the person who knew things, that stings.

But beginning is a skill, and like any skill it gets easier with reps. The trick is to make the first step embarrassingly small — small enough that you can't talk yourself out of it.

You are allowed to be new at something at any age. In fact it might be the most alive you'll feel all year.
