Four days built around two mountains: Rocky Mountain National Park and Mount Evans, with Red Rocks as the send-off.

### Day 1 — Thursday

**Meals:** Homemade lunch · dinner at a friend's place

**Stay:** Baymont by Wyndham, Denver International Airport

**Activity:**
- Flew into Denver, picked up a Turo rental, and explored the east side of the city
- Snack run at Walmart
- Dinner with friends

### Day 2 — Friday

**Meals:** Starbucks for breakfast · trail mix and snacks for lunch · The Rock Inn Mountain Tavern for dinner

**Stay:** Granby

**Activity:**
- Drove to Rocky Mountain National Park
- Views at Lilly Lake
- Hiked the Twin Sisters Trailhead (7.4 miles)
- Takeout from The Rock Inn Mountain Tavern
- Walked to Emerald Lake before driving on to Granby

### Day 3 — Saturday

**Meals:** Instant oatmeal for breakfast · Subway for lunch · Tommyknocker Brewery & Pub for dinner

**Stay:** Evergreen, Colorado

**Activity:**
- Grocery run in Granby
- Drove to Mount Evans, lunch at Echo Lake
- Summit Lake and the Mount Evans summit
- Hiked the M. Walter Pesman Trail
- Dinner at Tommyknocker Brewery & Pub

### Day 4 — Sunday

**Meals:** Vegan French toast from the Airbnb host · lunch at The Golden Mill · dinner at home

**Activity:**
- Red Rocks Park and Amphitheater
- Lunch at The Golden Mill
- Car wash and gas near Aurora before returning the rental and flying home
