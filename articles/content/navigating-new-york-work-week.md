A work week in New York, logged day by day — client site during the day, the city everywhere else.

### Day 1 — Saturday

**Meals:**
- Lunch: Mom's food
- Dinner: Ramen Ku-Raku — spicy tofu vegetable gyoza and ramen
- Stay: Airbnb

**Activity:**
- Flew into New York, Uber from JFK to Upper Manhattan

### Day 2 — Sunday

**Meals:**
- Lunch: Mom's packed fried rice
- Dinner: Screamer's Pizzeria (vegan pizza) and Van Leeuwen ice cream
- Stay: Airbnb

**Activity:**
- One-mile morning run around the neighborhood
- Evening at Edge at Hudson Yards
- Train to Brooklyn for pizza and ice cream

### Day 3 — Monday

**Meals:**
- Dinner: Beyond Sushi, with a friend
- Stay: Airbnb

**Activity:**
- One-mile morning run before the train into work

### Day 4 — Tuesday

**Meals:**
- Breakfast: Gregory's Coffee — coffee and a pastry
- Lunch: Beatnic
- Dinner: LadyBird
- Stay: Airbnb

**Activity:**
- Coffee with colleagues, then donuts from Dough on the way to the client site

### Day 5 — Wednesday

**Meals:**
- Breakfast: Caffe Vita — chai latte
- Lunch: Jajaja Mexicana
- Dinner: Chipotle (to-go)
- Stay: Airbnb

**Activity:**
- Hot yoga at Fierce Grace
- Worked from a colleague's apartment, then moved the afternoon to the Mexican restaurant — churros for the whole table
- Subway back to the office for a late night

### Day 6 — Thursday

**Meals:**
- Lunch: Beatnic
- Dinner: Thai
- Stay: Airbnb

**Activity:**
- Client site all day
- 30-minute meditation session at a sangha with a colleague

### Day 7 — Friday

**Meals:**
- Lunch: Leftover Thai
- Dinner: Vegan on the Fly
- Stay: Airbnb

**Activity:**
- Worked from the Airbnb
- Ice skating at Rockefeller Center, a stop at Adidas, and a walk through Times Square

### Day 8 — Saturday

**Meals:**
- Dinner: Mom's food, back home
- Stay: Home

**Activity:**
- Lyft to JFK and the flight home to Los Angeles
