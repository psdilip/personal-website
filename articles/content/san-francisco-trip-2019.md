Four days covering San Francisco end to end, with a side trip out to Yosemite in the middle.

### Day 1

**Stats:** 14.0 miles walked · parked at Alta Plaza Park

**Meals:** Breakfast at IHOP in Daly City · snacks for lunch · dinner at Om Indian Cuisine

**Stops:** Lyon Street Steps, Broadway Street, the Palace of Fine Arts, original Ghirardelli ice cream, Lombard Street, Fisherman's Wharf, Pier 39, a bay cruise, the Ferry Building, Chinatown, Union Square, City Hall, and the Painted Ladies

### Day 2

**Stats:** 6.0 miles walked · 5 hours of driving · parked near Marshall's Beach

**Meals:** Breakfast at Round House Cafe · lunch at In-N-Out · dinner at Palamuru Grill

**Stops:** Golden Gate Overlook, the Golden Gate Observation Deck, another viewpoint on the bridge, Battery Spencer, Crissy Field Overlook, Marshall's Beach, Lands End Lookout, Sutro Baths, the USS San Francisco Memorial, a drive through Golden Gate Park, the 16th Avenue Tiled Steps, Twin Peaks, Facebook HQ, and Stanford University

### Day 3

**Stats:** 1.4 miles walked · 9 hours of driving

**Meals:** Starbucks for breakfast · Burger King for lunch · Palamuru Grill again for dinner

**Stops:** A day trip out to Yosemite — Tunnel View, Yosemite Valley, and Yosemite Falls. Glacier Point was closed.

### Day 4

**Stats:** 12.9 miles walked · parked on Fulton Street

**Meals:** Krispy Kreme for breakfast · Beach Chalet Brewery and Restaurant for lunch · dinner somewhere in the airport

**Stops:** A full loop through Golden Gate Park — the Conservatory of Flowers, Robin Williams Meadow, the National AIDS Memorial Grove, the California Academy of Sciences, the Shakespeare Garden, the Japanese Tea Garden, and Strawberry Hill — then the fire pits at Ocean Beach before heading to the airport.
