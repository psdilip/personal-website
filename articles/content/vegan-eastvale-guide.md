Eight places in Eastvale worth knowing if you're eating plant-based, and exactly what to order at each one.

- **Luna Grill** — the Vegan Bowl (housemade falafel, lettuce, vegan tahini sauce) or the Falafel Plate with basmati rice and pita
- **JINYA Ramen Bar** — Spicy Creamy Vegan Ramen, Impossible Bun, Impossible Gyoza, Impossible Tacos
- **Subway** — the Hearty Multigrain Footlong, veggie patty, loaded with vegetables
- **Chipotle** — a bowl with rice, beans, veggies, sofritas, salsa, and avocado
- **Blaze Pizza** — a custom pie with vegan cheese, spicy chorizo, and whatever vegetables you want piled on
- **WaBa Grill** — the Plantspired Veggie Bowl, or the Tofu Bowl with grilled organic tofu
- **Dog Haus** — the SOOO Veggie, a beyond-sausage sandwich with avocado and arugula
- **Bushfire Kitchen** — the Grilled Veggies Bowl over jasmine or brown rice

Menus shift, so it's always worth confirming prep methods yourself if you're strict about what counts as vegan.
