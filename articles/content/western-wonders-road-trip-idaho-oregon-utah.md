Seven days, three states, one Turo rental car. A day-by-day log of a loop through Boise, the Wallowa Mountains, Twin Falls, and Salt Lake City.

### Day 1 — Thursday

**Meals:**
- Lunch: Qudoba at Seattle Airport
- Dinner: Mom's tomato fried rice
- Stay: Airbnb in Boise, ID

**Activity:**
- Flight from Ontario to Seattle
- Flight from Seattle to Boise
- Picked up the Turo rental car at the airport
- Checked into the Airbnb

*Drone shot of downtown Boise from Table Rock.*

### Day 2 — Friday

**Meals:**
- Breakfast: Strawberry banana with pea protein, and a berry cashew chia parfait at Nektar Juice Bar
- Lunch: Mom's tomato fried rice
- Dinner: Breakfast burrito from High Note Cafe
- Stay: Airbnb in Pendleton, Oregon

**Activity:**
- One-hour session at Orangetheory Fitness
- Table Rock hike from the Old Idaho Penitentiary site (1.5 hours total, 866 ft elevation gain)
- Drove to Pendleton, Oregon

### Day 3 — Saturday

**Meals:**
- Breakfast: Burger at Roosters Country Kitchen with a drink from Dutch Bros
- Dinner: Thai food at Thai Crystal
- Stay: Airbnb in Pendleton

**Activity:**
- Drove from Pendleton to Wallowa Lake
- Rode the tramway up to Mt. Howard
- Kayaked at Wallowa Lake

*Drone shot of Wallowa Lake and the surrounding mountain range.*

### Day 4 — Sunday

**Meals:**
- Lunch: Chipotle in Boise
- Dinner: Leftover Thai food
- Stay: Airbnb in Twin Falls, Idaho

**Activity:**
- Drove from Pendleton, Oregon to Twin Falls, Idaho
- Visited the Perrine Bridge

*Drone shot of the Perrine Memorial Bridge.*

### Day 5 — Monday

**Meals:**
- Breakfast: TruFuel acai bowl
- Lunch: 4 Roots Juice Bar
- Dinner: Prohibition restaurant
- Stay: A friend's place in Salt Lake City, Utah

**Activity:**
- Visited Shoshone Falls and Lake Cleveland
- Drove from Twin Falls to Salt Lake City

*Drone shot of Shoshone Falls and Lake Cleveland.*

### Day 6 — Tuesday

**Meals:**
- Breakfast: Leftover Prohibition
- Dinner: Noodles and Company
- Stay: Airbnb in Boise

**Activity:**
- Drove from Salt Lake City, Utah back to Boise, Idaho

### Day 7 — Wednesday

**Meals:**
- Lunch: Garbanzo Mediterranean Fresh at SLC Airport
- Dinner: Home
- Stay: Home

**Activity:**
- Checked out of the Airbnb
- Dropped off the Turo rental at the airport
- Flight from Boise to Salt Lake City
- Flight from Salt Lake City to Ontario
