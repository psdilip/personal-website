The idea

Passions, sent out in waves.

PassionWavesMedia is exactly what it sounds like — a way to send the things I care about out into the world and see who they reach. Some pieces will teach you how to do something. Some will take you somewhere. All of them are meant to leave you a little more curious than you were.

It starts with writing. Over time it'll grow into other forms of media, too — but the goal never changes: inspire someone, help someone, pass along something worth knowing.

— and if it lands with even one person, that's a wave worth making.
