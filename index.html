My Life....

Born in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, India. Came to US in 2005 with my family. Lived in California till high school and moved out to Salt Lake City to pursue my bachelors. Then travelled to Indianapolis and North Carolina for work and currently residing in Los Angeles, California.

I have worked in the cloud industry for nearly five years with a focus in the IoT space. Ever since I was introduced to Cloud, I was interested in its capabilities and possible applications as a solution to various current problems. I like to go above and beyond in the projects I work on, and understand the full architecture of how the each piece of the puzzle is applied. I am currently learning new technologies and indulging in activities outside of work that enable me to develop my creativity and live through purpose.
